The cycle of

Coir Pith Manufacturing

Husk Collection

High quality procurement direct from the farmers.

Washing

Wash with fresh water to bring down the PH level to desired factor.

Sieving

Remove chunks and others impurities.

Drying

Dry in direct sun light till the desired moisture and PH level achieved.

Screening

Remove sand and other undesired particles.

Blocks

Using hydraulic machine, compress the pith to various block sizes.

Quality Check

Quality inspectors ensure samples from every batch meeting the expected standards.

Packaging

As per the customer packaging expected standards.

Transportation

Palletized customer defined shipping standards.
