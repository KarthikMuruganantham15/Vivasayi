About Vivasai

We deliver more than your expectation

Vivasai Agro Products Private Limited – Started with an intention to enhance the livelihood of farmers by adapting cutting edge technology and value added manufacturing facility to deliver innovative products globally.

Vision

“Our vision is to build environmentally sustainable agricultural products and introduce them back in our daily lives to reduce global warming and lead the coming generations into a safer and stronger earth”

Mission

“To manufacture agriculture-based utility products that are on par or better than their synthetic counter parts with top focus on safety, security, sustainability and environment. Our products and brands will be synonymous with healthy life not just for our customers but also for the eco-system they live in.”
